# Government debt Line chart
General government debt-to-GDP ratio measures the gross debt of the general government as a percentage of GDP. It is a key indicator for the sustainability of government finance. The graph shows the proportion owned by each country from 1995 to 2019. At the same time, JPN is the highest and EST is the lowest.
